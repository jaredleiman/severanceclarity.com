FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS.

Is this legal advice?
No. SeveranceClarity is an educational document explainer service. We explain what your agreement says in plain English. We are not attorneys, do not provide legal advice, and do not establish an attorney-client relationship. If you need legal advice, please consult a licensed employment attorney in your state.
How long does it take to receive my report?
We deliver all reports within 24–48 hours of receiving your payment and completed intake form. Most are delivered sooner. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately after submitting.
What file formats do you accept?
We accept PDF, Word (.doc / .docx), and image files (JPG, PNG) of your severance agreement. If you received a physical copy, a clear phone photo works fine.
Is my document kept private?
Yes. Your document is used solely to prepare your explainer report and is not shared with any third parties. We treat all submissions as confidential.
What if I'm over 40? Does that change anything?
Yes — federal law (the ADEA and OWBPA) gives employees over 40 additional protections, including a mandatory 21-day review period and a 7-day revocation window after signing. Our Full Breakdown includes a specific checklist to verify whether your agreement meets these federal requirements.
Which report should I choose?
If you want a clear summary of the main clauses, the Quick Explainer ($147) is a great fit. If you want every clause explained in full detail, red flags identified, and a list of questions to raise with an attorney, choose the Full Breakdown ($347).
What if I have questions after receiving my report?
Reach us at hello@severanceclarity.com. For legal questions about your specific situation, always consult a licensed employment attorney.
