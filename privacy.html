PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an order, we collect your name, email address, and the severance agreement document you upload. We may also collect standard web analytics data such as browser type and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to prepare and deliver your requested explainer report. Your email address is used to send your completed report and any necessary communications about your order. We do not use your information for marketing without your explicit consent.

3. Document Handling

The severance agreement document you submit is used exclusively to generate your explainer report. Your document is not sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. We treat all submitted documents as strictly confidential.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Document files are processed securely and are not retained beyond what is necessary to fulfill your order.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our business, including Stripe for payment processing, Jotform for secure file intake, and Resend for transactional email delivery. Each of these services maintains their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools to understand site traffic and improve our service. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@severanceclarity.com. We will respond within 30 days. You may also request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@severanceclarity.com.
